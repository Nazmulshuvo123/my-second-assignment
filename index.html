BMI Calculator

BMI estimates body fat based on height and weight. It's a simple tool but has limitations,
as it doesn't account for muscle mass or other factors.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
0.0 and Above Obese

Calculate your BMI

Divide your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared.
This number is your BMI, which gives a roughestimate of your body fat.
